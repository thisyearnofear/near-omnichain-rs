About Omni Bridge

Omni Bridge enables seamless cross-chain USDC transfers using advanced cryptographic signatures. Built on cutting-edge technology, it eliminates the need for traditional bridges while maintaining security and decentralization.

⚡ Lightning fast transfers (~30 seconds)
🔒 Bank-grade security with cryptographic proofs
💎 Ultra-low fees (under $0.05)
🌐 No wrapped tokens or complex bridges

Terms & Information

🛡️ Security

  • All transactions use cryptographic signatures
  • No private keys are stored or transmitted
  • Smart contracts are audited and open source
  • Non-custodial - you control your funds

💰 Fees

  • Bridge fee: Free during beta
  • Network fees: ~$0.02 (Base gas)
  • No hidden charges or markups
  • Transparent fee structure

⚠️ Risks

  • Smart contract risk (audited but not zero)
  • Network congestion may delay transfers
  • Beta software - use at your own risk
  • Always verify recipient addresses
